Under the Hood

Built right.

GraphQL-Powered

Uses the Shopify Admin GraphQL API with paginated resource fetching. Analyzes SEO fields, content length, and image attributes across your entire catalog efficiently.

Zero Storefront Impact

All analysis happens server-side via API. No JavaScript injected into your theme. No impact on page load times or Lighthouse scores. Your store stays fast.

Privacy-First

Only reads store content metadata -- no customer data is ever accessed. SEO analysis is performed on product titles, descriptions, and image attributes only.

Permissions

What SeoAudit accesses.

We request only the scopes necessary for the app to function. Nothing extra.

read_products
Read product titles, descriptions, meta fields, and image alt text for SEO analysis.
read_content
Read pages, blog articles, and collections to audit meta tags, content depth, and SEO fields.
