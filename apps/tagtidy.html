Features

Four types of tag issues,
one cleanup tool.

Case Duplicate Detection

Finds tags like "Summer-Sale" and "summer-sale" that differ only by capitalization. These split your products across what should be a single tag. Merge them into one canonical tag with a single click.

"Summer-Sale"case dup
"summer-sale"merge →

Near-Duplicate Detection

Uses smart fuzzy matching to catch typos and variants like "t-shirt" vs "tshirt" or "running-shoe" vs "runningshoe". Clean up inconsistencies automatically and unify your tag vocabulary.

"t-shirt"dist: 1
"tshirt"merge →

Single-Use Tag Cleanup

Identifies tags used on only one product. These clutter your filtering and make collections harder to manage. Delete or merge them easily to keep your tag list meaningful and actionable.

"limited-edition-2023"1 product
"winter-promo-old"1 product

Unused Tag Removal

Finds tags that exist in your store but aren't applied to any products. Remove dead weight to keep your tag taxonomy clean and your collection filters relevant.

"clearance-old"0 products
"holiday-2021"0 products
Under the Hood

Built right.

GraphQL & Pagination

Uses the Shopify Admin GraphQL API with cursor-based pagination to fetch every product tag in your store. Batched product count queries with built-in rate limiting keep things fast and safe.

Fuzzy Matching

Levenshtein distance algorithm powers intelligent near-duplicate detection. Catches typos, missing hyphens, and subtle variants that manual review would miss. Configurable sensitivity threshold.

Atomic Tag Operations

Merge and remove operations use Shopify's tagsAdd and tagsRemove mutations for atomic updates. Zero storefront impact — all analysis and cleanup happens server-side.

Permissions

What TagTidy accesses.

We request only the scopes necessary for the app to function. Nothing extra. Privacy-first: only product tags are read, no customer data.

read_products
Read product tags and product counts to detect duplicates, near-duplicates, single-use, and unused tags across your store.
write_products
Add and remove tags on products during merge and cleanup operations. Only modifies tags you explicitly approve.
