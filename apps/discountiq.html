Features

Four discount strategies,
one app.

Volume Discounts

Set per-product tiered pricing. Buy 5, get 10% off. Buy 10, get 20% off. Configure as many tiers as you need per product. Customers automatically get the best price based on their cart quantity.

qty >= 5-10%
qty >= 10-20%
qty >= 25-30%

Order Tier Discounts

Reward bigger carts with tiered discounts based on order subtotal. Encourage customers to spend more with progressively better deals at each threshold.

spend $50+-5%
spend $100+-10%
spend $200+-15%

VIP Customer Discounts

Automatically give extra discounts to customers with specific tags. Reward your most loyal customers, wholesale buyers, or any segment you define using Shopify customer tags.

tag: "vip-gold"-15%
tag: "wholesale"-25%

Shipping Discounts

Offer free or discounted shipping when customers hit a spending threshold. One of the most effective conversion tools — remove the #1 reason for cart abandonment.

subtotal >= $75free shipping
Under the Hood

Built right.

Shopify Functions

Discounts execute on Shopify's edge infrastructure using WebAssembly-powered Functions. No external API calls at checkout. Sub-millisecond execution.

Zero Storefront Impact

No JavaScript injected into your theme. No impact on Lighthouse scores. Your store stays fast because discount logic runs server-side on Shopify's infrastructure.

Privacy-First

We store nothing beyond OAuth sessions. Customer tags are read at runtime, never persisted. Discount configs live as Shopify metafields. Full GDPR compliance.

Permissions

What DiscountIQ accesses.

We request only the scopes necessary for the app to function. Nothing extra.

read_discounts, write_discounts
Create, update, activate, deactivate, and delete discount configurations.
read_products
Display product names and variants when configuring volume discounts.
read_customers
Read customer tags at checkout for VIP discount eligibility. Tags are never stored.
