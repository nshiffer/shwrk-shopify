Open DiscountIQ from your Shopify admin sidebar. Click "Create Discount" and choose your discount type (volume, order tier, VIP, or shipping). Follow the step-by-step wizard to configure tiers, thresholds, and conditions. Preview your discount before saving.
LinkGuard scans all your product descriptions, pages, blog articles, and navigation menus for internal links. It then checks each link against your store's resources using the Shopify API to detect 404 errors, links to archived products, and links to unpublished content. You can fix issues directly with one-click URL redirects or by editing the source content.
SeoAudit scans all your products, pages, blog articles, and collections for SEO issues. It checks for missing meta titles and descriptions, overly long titles, short descriptions, missing image alt text, and thin content (pages with fewer than 300 words). You get an overall SEO health score from 0-100 and a prioritized list of issues to fix.
TagTidy detects four types of tag issues: Case duplicates (e.g., "Summer-Sale" vs "summer-sale"), Near-duplicates (typos like "t-shirt" vs "tshirt"), Single-use tags (tags on only one product), and Unused tags (tags not on any products). You can merge duplicates into a canonical tag, delete unused tags, or dismiss issues you want to keep.
