Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

Shwrk ("we", "us", or "our") operates Shopify applications distributed through the Shopify App Store. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you install and use our apps.

1. Information We Collect

Our apps collect only the minimum data necessary to function. Specifically:

  • Store information: Your Shopify store domain, used to identify your installation and authenticate API requests.
  • OAuth session data: Access tokens and session identifiers provided by Shopify during the app installation process. These are required for the app to communicate with your store.
  • User identity (online sessions): First name, last name, email, locale, and account role of the logged-in Shopify admin user, as provided by Shopify during authentication.

We do not collect, store, or process customer personal data. Our apps interact with Shopify APIs at runtime to read product, discount, and customer tag information, but this data is not persisted in our systems.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • Authenticating your Shopify store and maintaining your app session.
  • Providing the app's core functionality (creating and managing discounts, processing Shopify Function configurations).
  • Responding to Shopify webhook events related to your store.

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties. We do not use your data for marketing, analytics, or profiling purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Session data is stored in a secure database accessible only to our application servers. Access tokens are stored encrypted at rest. We follow industry standard security practices including HTTPS-only communication and Shopify's HMAC webhook verification.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

  • On uninstall: When you uninstall one of our apps, all session data for your store is automatically deleted via the app/uninstalled webhook.
  • Shop redaction: Within 48 hours of uninstall, Shopify sends a shop redaction request. We delete any remaining data associated with your store within 30 days of receiving this request.
  • Customer redaction: If Shopify sends a customer redaction request, we acknowledge it immediately. Since we do not store customer personal data, no further action is required.
  • Customer data request: If Shopify sends a customer data request, we acknowledge it and confirm that no customer personal data is stored in our systems.

5. GDPR and Privacy Law Compliance

We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and other applicable data privacy laws. We implement all mandatory Shopify compliance webhooks:

  • customers/data_request — We respond to requests for customer data access.
  • customers/redact — We respond to requests to delete customer data.
  • shop/redact — We respond to requests to delete all shop data.

Since our apps do not store customer personal data, data subject requests are acknowledged and no additional deletion is necessary beyond session cleanup.

6. Third-Party Services

Our apps interact exclusively with the Shopify Admin GraphQL API and Shopify Functions runtime. We do not integrate with any third-party analytics, tracking, advertising, or data processing services.

7. Cookies and Tracking

Our apps do not set cookies on your storefront or track your customers. Within the Shopify admin, our embedded apps use Shopify's session token authentication, which does not rely on third-party cookies.

8. App-Specific Information

DiscountIQ

DiscountIQ creates and manages automatic discounts using Shopify Functions. It accesses:

  • Discount data (read/write) — To create, update, and manage discount configurations.
  • Product data (read only) — To display product information when configuring volume discounts.
  • Customer data (read only) — To read customer tags at runtime for VIP discount eligibility. Customer tags are read in real-time via Shopify Functions and are never stored.

All discount configurations are stored as Shopify metafields on the discount resource itself, not in our external database.

LinkGuard

LinkGuard scans your store content for broken internal links. It accesses:

  • Product data (read only) — To scan product descriptions for broken links and check product status (active, archived, draft).
  • Content data (read only) — To scan pages and blog articles for broken links.
  • Navigation data (read only) — To scan navigation menus for broken links.
  • URL redirects (write) — To create URL redirects as a fix for 404 errors, when requested by the merchant.

LinkGuard stores scan results (broken link issue data) locally to track resolution status. No customer data is accessed or stored.

TagTidy

TagTidy audits and cleans up product tags. It accesses:

  • Product data (read) — To fetch all product tags and determine how many products use each tag.
  • Product data (write) — To add or remove tags from products when the merchant performs merge, rename, or delete actions.

TagTidy stores audit results (tag issue data) locally to track resolution status. No customer data is accessed or stored.

SeoAudit

SeoAudit scans your store for SEO issues. It accesses:

  • Product data (read only) — To analyze product meta titles, descriptions, content length, and image alt text.
  • Content data (read only) — To analyze pages, blog articles, and collections for SEO completeness.

SeoAudit stores audit results (SEO issue data and scores) locally to track resolution status. No customer data is accessed or stored.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify affected merchants of any material changes through the Shopify admin or via email. The "last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when this policy was most recently revised.
